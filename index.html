Installation Guide

1. Install directly via LiveContainer (LcInstaller)

If you use LiveContainer, you can install LinkRelay directly:

  1. Open LcInstaller on your iOS device.
  2. Tap the source management button, and add the following URL:
    https://aoi.github.io/aoirepo/apps.json
  3. Select **LinkRelay** from the list and tap **Install** to add it to your LiveContainer.

2. Add to AltStore or SideStore

To enable updates directly through AltStore or SideStore:

  1. Tap the **Add to AltStore** or **Add to SideStore** button at the top of this page.
  2. Confirm the addition of the source in the app.
  3. Download and install LinkRelay directly from the **Browse** tab.
